Cinnamon toast recipe

Easy and tasty Cinnamon toast

This is a very easy recipe for some delicious cinnamon toast!

It is very simple and requires few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Spread butter on toast.
  2. Toast the bread to desired level.
  3. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
  4. Enjoy!
